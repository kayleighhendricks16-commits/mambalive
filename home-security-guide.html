Home Security Guide

Your home should be a safe haven. Mamba Security's Home Security solutions combine technology, prevention, and fast response – serving Edenvale, Kempton Park, Benoni, Primrose and across Gauteng.

Perimeter Protection

Securing your property's outer boundaries

  • Secure Fences, Walls, and Gates

    Ensure all perimeter barriers are in good condition. Install anti-climbing measures like spikes or electric fencing. All gates should be lockable and monitored.

  • Clear Visibility Lines

    Trim shrubs and trees to reduce hiding spots. Maintain clear visibility around your property perimeter to eliminate blind spots for potential intruders.

  • Security Signage

    Display visible Mamba Security signage. This acts as a deterrent and clearly communicates that your property is professionally protected.

  • Boundary Sensors

    Install perimeter sensors and beams that detect movement along your property boundaries before intruders reach your home.

Access Control

Controlling who enters your property

  • Reinforce Entry Points

    Strengthen doors, sliding doors, and garage doors with security gates or roll-down shutters. Use high-quality locks and deadbolts on all entry points.

  • Key Management

    Control who has keys and remote access. Change locks when keys are lost or when moving into a new property. Never hide keys outside.

  • Staff Security Training

    Train household staff on security procedures, including checking credentials of visitors and reporting suspicious activity immediately.

  • Visitor Verification

    Always verify visitors before opening gates or doors. Use intercom systems or CCTV to identify visitors before granting access.

Alarm Systems & Monitoring

Professional protection and rapid response

  • Professional Monitoring

    Install professionally monitored alarm systems that connect directly to our 24 hour control center for immediate response to any breach.

  • Panic Buttons

    Place panic buttons in key locations like bedrooms, living areas, and garages. Consider wearable panic buttons for personal protection.

  • 24 hour Armed Response

    Ensure your alarm system is linked to Mamba's rapid armed response teams, providing immediate assistance when needed.

  • Regular Testing

    Test your alarm system monthly and ensure regular maintenance. Update your contact information with the monitoring center.

  • Smart Home Integration

    Integrate security systems with smart home technology for remote monitoring and control via mobile devices.

Lighting & Visibility

Illuminating your property for safety

  • Motion-Activated Lighting

    Install motion-sensor lights around perimeter areas, entrances, and dark corners. These deter intruders and improve visibility.

  • Eliminate Blind Spots

    Identify and eliminate blind spots around your property. Ensure all areas are visible from windows or security cameras.

  • Maintain Outdoor Lighting

    Regularly check and replace outdoor light bulbs. Consider solar-powered lights for areas without electrical access.

  • Timed Indoor Lights

    Use timers for indoor lights when away to give the appearance that someone is home, deterring potential burglars.

Personal Awareness

Safe habits for you and your family

  • Safe Arrival and Departure

    Practice safe arrival and departure habits. Be aware of surroundings when entering or leaving your property.

  • Social Media Caution

    Avoid broadcasting absences or travel plans on social media. Wait until after returning home to post vacation photos.

  • Family Emergency Protocols

    Teach all family members emergency protocols, including how to use panic buttons and where to go in case of emergency.

  • Neighborhood Cooperation

    Develop good relationships with neighbors and participate in neighborhood watch programs. Look out for each other.

Security Assessment

A professional security assessment is the first step toward effective protection. Mamba Security conducts thorough evaluations to identify risks and provide tailored solutions in Edenvale, Kempton Park, Benoni, Primrose and across Gauteng.

Property & Site Evaluation

Understanding your environment is critical

  • Inspection of Access Points and Perimeters

    Comprehensive evaluation of all entry and exit points, fencing, gates, and perimeter security to identify vulnerabilities.

  • Identification of Blind Spots and Vulnerabilities

    Detailed analysis of your property to identify security blind spots and areas vulnerable to unauthorized access or surveillance.

  • Evaluation of Fencing, Gates, and Lighting

    Professional assessment of physical security measures including fencing integrity, gate security, and illumination levels.

System & Technology Review

Ensuring systems perform when needed

  • Alarm and CCTV System Testing

    Comprehensive testing of existing alarm systems, CCTV cameras, sensors, and monitoring equipment for optimal performance.

  • Review of Response Procedures

    Evaluation of emergency response protocols, alarm verification processes, and communication systems with security providers.

  • Maintenance and Upgrade Recommendations

    Professional recommendations for system maintenance, technology upgrades, and integration of modern security solutions.

Risk Analysis & Reporting

Knowledge drives better security decisions

  • Threat Identification and Prioritisation

    Systematic identification of potential security threats specific to your location and property type, with risk prioritization.

  • Assessment of Crime Exposure

    Analysis of local crime trends, historical data, and vulnerability to specific types of criminal activity in your area.

  • Detailed Professional Reporting

    Comprehensive written report detailing findings, risk levels, and prioritized recommendations with actionable insights.

Custom Security Recommendations

No two properties are the same

  • Tailored Security Strategies

    Custom security plans designed specifically for your property, lifestyle, and security requirements, not generic templates.

  • Equipment and System Upgrades

    Specific recommendations for security technology upgrades, equipment placement, and integration with existing systems.

  • Implementation Planning

    Step-by-step implementation plans with timelines, budget considerations, and phased approaches to security improvements.

Ongoing Security Review

Security evolves with risk

  • Regular Reassessments

    Scheduled follow-up assessments to evaluate security measures, adapt to changing circumstances, and ensure continuous protection.

  • Continuous Improvement Strategies

    Proactive approach to security enhancement with regular updates, technology reviews, and adaptation to emerging threats.

  • Security Performance Monitoring

    Ongoing monitoring of security system performance and incident tracking to identify areas for improvement and optimization.
